About the Firm

Berger Holdings is a private investment firm based in Phoenix, Arizona, with a focus on real estate, hospitality, and entertainment.

The firm invests in direct real estate assets and operating companies through equity and credit structures. Through its affiliates, Berger Holdings owns or controls more than $1 billion in hospitality assets.
