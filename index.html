About the Firm

Where Capital Meets
Craft

Berger Holdings is a private real estate investment firm specializing in the acquisition and strategic repositioning of luxury hotel assets.

We identify opportunities in premier hospitality markets where capital, operational expertise, and brand positioning can be brought together to unlock lasting value. Our approach combines rigorous underwriting with a deep understanding of the guest experience — treating each asset as both a financial instrument and a hospitality enterprise. From select-service repositioning to full-scale resort development, we invest where quality endures.
